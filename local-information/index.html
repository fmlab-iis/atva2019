Humanities and Social Sciences Building (HSSB) is numbered by 24 in the Academia Sinica map guide.

Hotel

The nearest hotel is Green World Hotel Nangang (4 star). For conference attendees, the room rates are as follows:

  • Standard (2 single or 1 double): TWD 2,600
  • Superior (2 single or 1 double): TWD 2,900
  • Deluxe Triple (triple only): TWD 4,500
  • Executive (2 single or 1 double): TWD 3,600

To make your reservation at Green World Hotel Nangang, please fill this form and send the form to the hotel by email (Reservation2@gwh.global) or fax (+886-2-2531-0857).

There are some other options.

  • Premium option:
    • Courtyard by Marriott Taipei, Nangang District (5 stars)
    • Howard Hotel Taipei, Daan District (5 stars)
  • Economy option:
    • Activity Center within Academia Sinica
      • A limited number of free shared twin-bed rooms are available for student registration. Please submit your request using this Google form after registration and payment. Any request made before registration or payment will be ignored. A preferred roommate can be specified in the Google form. We will try our best to fulfill every roommate request but it is not guaranteed.
      • We have reserved some single-bed rooms and twin-bed rooms with a lower price.
        • Use this Google form to book a single-bed room (TWD 1,100 per night).
        • Use this Google form to book a twin-bed room (TWD 1,400 per night).
      • All the booking requests must be made before October 8 (GMT+8) and will be processed in submission order. We will send an email later for the confirmation of room availability.

Transportation

From Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport to Academia Sinica

  • Take Taoyuan Airport MRT from Airport Terminal 1 (A12) or Airport Terminal 2 (A13) to Taipei Main Station (A1)
  • Take Taipei MRT Bannan Line from Taipei Main Station (BL12) to Nangang (BL22), get out from Exit 2, turn right and find a bus stop
  • Take Bus 270 from MRT Nangang to Academia Sinica

The bus stop close to HSSB is Academia Sinica. Below is a route from Academia Sinica bus stop to HSSB.
